How to Book
Please choose your preferred escape room and follow the booking instructions on our website. It's easy and quick to secure your spot for a spine-tingling adventure!
Jester's Game
Enter the whimsical yet eerie world of the Jester, a master of playful deceit. This escape room is perfect for beginners or those looking for a lighthearted challenge with a creepy twist. As you navigate through the Jester's lair, you'll encounter a series of quirky puzzles and riddles designed to test your wits and keep you on your toes. The Jester's laugh echoes through the halls, adding to the suspense. Can you outsmart this mischievous clown and escape before the final joke is on you?

Carnaval of Horrors
Step right up to the Carnaval of Horrors, where every attraction hides a dark secret. This room offers a medium level of difficulty, making it ideal for those seeking a thrilling yet manageable challenge. As you wander through the carnival grounds, you'll face eerie performers, sinister games, and spine-chilling puzzles that require keen observation and quick thinking. The atmosphere is thick with tension, and every corner holds a new fright. Can you survive the horrors of the carnival and find your way out before becoming part of the show?
Book
The Nightmare
Dare to face The Nightmare, our most intense and challenging escape room. This experience is not for the faint of heart. As you step into this dark and twisted world, you'll be plunged into a realm where your worst fears come to life. The puzzles are complex, the atmosphere is overwhelmingly terrifying, and the pressure is immense. Every decision counts as you race against time to escape the clutches of your nightmares. Only the bravest and most skilled will manage to break free from this relentless terror. Are you up for the ultimate challenge?
