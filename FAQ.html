Questions?
Booking
Can we book a private session for a group event or party?

Yes, you can book a private session for your group event or party. Please contact us directly to discuss availability and special arrangements.

How far in advance should we book our escape room experience?

We recommend booking your escape room experience at least a week in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekends and holidays.

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can change or cancel your booking. Please refer to our cancellation policy for specific details and contact us as soon as possible to make any changes.

Personal
What are the age requirements for participating in the escape rooms?

Our escape rooms are designed for ages 12 and up. Participants under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

Is it safe for people with medical conditions to participate?

Our escape rooms are designed to be safe, but if you have a medical condition, please consult with your doctor before participating. Inform our staff of any concerns so we can accommodate your needs.

Do you offer any accommodations for people with disabilities?

Yes, we strive to make our escape rooms accessible to everyone. Please contact us in advance to discuss any specific accommodations you may need.
